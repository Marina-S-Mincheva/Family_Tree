Get started with your own Family tree

Step 1:

You have to login/register if you want to create your own Family tree. It is for free, so do not hesitate to create your account.

Step 2:

And now, after authentication you can create the first member in your Family tree. Remember that the first one have to be the oldest in your Family tree.

You have an option to look at other Family trees in case you are looking for someone.

Step 3:

Let's get started and fill out the first form. You can choose name, gender, relations and to provide personal facebook link. Of course you can save and your favourite pics.

Step 4:

And just in a second you can see your Family tree to become bigger and bigger.

Step 5:

After you have created members, with left mouse click you can add new member,

clear a member or to view/correct member details.

Step 6:

And after you are ready with collecting memories, just with one click you can print your Family tree and save it forever. It could be a great picture on your wall or the perfect gift.
Take your time, create your Family Tree and discover your roots.
